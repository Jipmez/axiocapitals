Frequently Asked Questions

When you sponsor a project through Crowdyvest, you get periodic updates in the form of written reports, images and videos showing the progress being made at different stages of the project.

Your initial sponsorship and returns will be paid at the end of the project cycle into the account details on your profile. You can click on 'edit profile' on your dashboard to update your bank details and other information.

Each of our projects have a fixed cycle which is communicated in the project description page before you sponsor. Since projects have commenced, you will be unable to terminate your sponsorship before the agreed timeframe. However, when you receive your payback at the end of the project cycle, you can choose to sponsor another project, subject to availability.
